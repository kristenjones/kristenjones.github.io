NOT #MeToo

"awareness to teen dating violence and consent"

NOT #MeToo is an event that highlights the importance of knowing and preventing teen dating violence. As a victim who has been personally violated on a date, I find it important for people to know more about this issue. Currently, the hash-tag #MeToo has been trending, with women sharing their stories about assault. However, the phrase 'NOT #MeToo' will be used as a way to motivate and prevent girls from ever having to add to the hash-tag!
